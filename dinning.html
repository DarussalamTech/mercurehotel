Experience the Taste
Mercure Value welcomes guests with its contemporary décor

Les Bernaches Restaurant

Mercure Value welcomes guests with its contemporary décor abounding a pleasant and relaxed ambience.

 

It is the only place in town to sample the lavish French Breakfast, offering an extensive choice of quality international food and beverage items, as well as local specialties and in addition – 'made to order' favorites. All these temptations are arranged on a beautifully decorated buffet.

 

Dine on our sumptuous buffet or exquisite a la carte menu in the evening. The choice is extensive, well-balanced, international and includes French cuisine, vegetarian food and fine delicacies. During the summer the Hotel Deck offers a pleasant outdoor sitting.

Value Coffee Shop

Value Coffee Shop located at Ground floor in hotel lobby is the ideal place for gatherings, colleagues and friends can relax and enjoy cocktails and traditional arabic coffee.
