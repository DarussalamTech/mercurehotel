Le Club Accor Hotels - Loyalty Programme

The Le Club Accorhotels programme lets members earn points around the world on all expenditure in participating Accor hotels. Points are valid for life and have no expiry date, as long as members stay at a participating Accor hotel at least once a year. Depending on how often used, the Le Club Accorhotels loyalty card enables members to earn more and more points and enjoy exclusive advantages by gaining access to the Le Club Accorhotels Silver, Gold and Platinum elite statuses.

When transferring to a higher status, Le Club Accorhotels members multiply their points!

- Exclusive special attention and privileges during each stay: personalised welcome, welcome drink, room upgrade, private sales, etc.
Members can also earn points with Le Club Accorhotels partners: Club Med, Europcar, Lenôtre, Shell and more.

Highly Rewarded Loyalty: Flexible Rewards

From 2,000 points, members can spend their points on a wide choice of rewards:

- Members can directly deduct points from the cost of their stay when booking on Accorhotels.com or participating Accor brand websites

- Le Club Accorhotels selects dream holidays and VIP events especially for its members, with exceptional conditions, only available for booking with loyalty points

- Le Club Accorhotels members can also convert their points into hotel vouchers or partner vouchers of a value of €40.

- Le Club Accorhotels points can be freely transformed into air miles from a wide choice of flight company loyalty programmes.

- Members can also choose to convert all or some of their points into donations to charity.

To join, simply email us or let one of our friendly receptionist know when you next visit the hotel.
